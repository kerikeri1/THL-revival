Paranoia is not a disorder, it's a feature.

Every single thing running on your machine needs to justify its existence. Why is it here? When does it run? How does it work? No answer? No party.

No network you didn't personally approve, no process you didn't explicitly invite, no data touching a disk you don't own. Everything in RAM, everything ephemeral, everything yours.

THL Revival doesn't want to be "secure" like those other distros with their fancy frameworks and compliance checklists. That's not the point. The point is that you, the person reading this, should have total control over every bit running on your hardware.

keep being paranoid, keep having no trust ;P
